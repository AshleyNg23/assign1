Land of the Lustrous

About the story

In the mysterious future, crystalline organisms called Gems inhabit a world that has been destroyed by six meteors. Each Gem is assigned a role in order to fight against the Lunarians, a species who attacks them in order to shatter their bodies and use them as decorations. Phosphophyllite, also known as Phos, is a young and fragile Gem who dreams of helping their friends in the war effort. Instead, they are told to compile an encyclopedia because of their delicate condition. We follow Phos' journey in finding their life purpose.

Phosphophylite

Being 300 years old, they are the youngest among the gems. Their hardness is 3.5, low compared to most of the other gems. They are brittle and weak so they are not suited for battle. Despite being clumsy and ineffective they were given the job of completing an encyclopedia. They want to find new job for Cinnabar, who patrols at night and lives distantly from the other gems.

Cinnabar

Cinnabar is isolated from others because their body produces a poison. They have a gloomy personality and a job patrolling the night. Cinn's hardness is only 2.

Diamond

They want to be like Bort and tend to say odd things. Their hardness is 10. Diamond is gentle and friendly to everyone. They have a special type of fighting, where they use the sides of their sword to reflect the Lunarian's arrows.
